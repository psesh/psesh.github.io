Bio

I am a Research Fellow in the Department of Mathematics (Statistics Section) at Imperial College London working with Andrew Duncan. I am also the Group Leader in Aeronautics in the Data-Centric Engineering Programme at The Alan Turing Institute, working with Mark Girolami.

My research is focused on aerothermal statistics, which broadly is the application of machine learning and computational statistics to solve challenging problems pertaining to aerothermal measurements, fluid simulations, and sensor systems. My group's research is funded by Rolls-Royce plc, EPSRC and the Lloyd's Register Foundation.
