Pranay Seshadri

Bio

I am a Research Fellow in the Department of Mathematics (Statistics Section) at Imperial College London. I am the Founder of Effective Quadratures, and also the Group Leader in Aeronautics in the Data-Centric Engineering Programme at The Alan Turing Institute.

My research is focused on aerothermal statistics, which broadly is the application of machine learning and computational statistics to solve challenging problems pertaining to aerothermal measurements and sensor systems. My group's research is funded by Rolls-Royce plc, EPSRC and the Lloyd's Register Foundation.
