Research directions.

  • Geometry and performance visualization of aeronautic assets in virtual reality.
    Key words: digital twin, virtual reality, computer-aided design, engines, aircrafts, airports, human-computer interaction, visual analytics, immersive analytics.
  • Improved aerothermal understanding from sparse measurements.
    Key words: Bayesian performance assessments, spatial field approximation, metrology, anomaly detection, machine learning.
  • Dimension reduction in aeronautic simulation, design and manufacturing.
    Key words: transonic fans, probes, active subspace performance maps, blade envelopes, photogrammetry, pedigree design rules, ridge-enhanced trust-region optimization.

Application areas of interest.

aero-engines, fans, compressors, turbines, jets, electric propulsion, urban air taxis.
